Why me ?
You might ask!

My name is Andrei Rusen, and I am a former professional athlete who spent 18 years of my life as a competitive swimmer. During my career, I had the honour of representing my country in numerous national, European, and world championships, and I am proud to have achieved many notable accomplishments in the field of swimming.

After my athletic career, I decided to channel my passion for fitness and sports into helping others achieve their dreams in this domain. I am a licensed sports and motor performance professional, having completed three years of rigorous study to earn my credentials as a professional fitness coach, swimming coach, and physical trainer. Additionally, I hold a master's degree in sport performance, which I earned after two years of intense analysis and study.

Throughout my academic and professional journey, I have also studied physical therapy for two years, and I have been practising it alongside my other areas of expertise. I am committed to staying up-to-date on the latest developments in this field and integrating them into my work with clients.

Currently, I am proud to be a member of the Romanian Swimming and Modern Pentathlon Federation, where I have been working for the past two years. In this role, I have had the opportunity to collaborate on the creation of European and National level competitions, manage databases, and analyse athletic performance data. I am also working closely with the Romanian Olympic and Sports Committee to support the development of the next generation of athletes.

My dedication to my profession and my commitment to helping others achieve their goals have been a driving force throughout my life. I believe that everyone has the potential to achieve their perfect body and unlock their full athletic potential with the right training and guidance. Whether you are an aspiring athlete or simply someone who wants to improve their fitness level, I am here to help you reach your goals and realize your full potential.

With best regards,

Andrei Rusen
